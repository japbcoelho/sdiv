\section{Basic Usage}

\subsection{Simple Division}

Division of 97 by 5:
\[\sdiv{97}{5}\]

Division of 100 by 7:
\[\sdiv{100}{7}\]

\subsection{With Circle Highlighting}

Same divisions with circles highlighting the remainders:
\[\sdiv[circle]{97}{5}\]

\[\sdiv[circle]{100}{7}\]

\subsection{With Arrow}

Arrow showing reading direction (useful for base conversion):
\[\sdiv[arrow]{97}{5}\]

\[\sdiv[arrow]{100}{7}\]

\subsection{Both Options Combined}

\[\sdiv[circle,arrow]{97}{5}\]

\[\sdiv[circle,arrow]{100}{7}\]

\subsection{With Underline Highlighting}

Underline highlighting using the uline option:
\[\sdiv[uline]{97}{5}\]

\[\sdiv[uline]{100}{7}\]

\subsection{Underline with Arrow}

\[\sdiv[uline,arrow]{97}{5}\]

\subsection{All Three Options Combined}

Circle, underline, and arrow together:
\[\sdiv[circle,uline,arrow]{97}{5}\]

\section{Base Conversion}

\subsection{Binary Conversion}

Converting 255 to binary (read remainders from bottom to top):
\[\sdiv[circle,arrow]{255}{2}\]

Result: $255_{10} = 11111111_2$

\subsection{Octal Conversion}

Converting 97 to octal:
\[\sdiv[circle,arrow]{97}{8}\]

Result: $97_{10} = 141_8$

\subsection{Hexadecimal Conversion}

Converting 1000 to hexadecimal:
\[\sdiv[circle,arrow]{1000}{16}\]

Result: $1000_{10} = 3\text{E}8_{16}$ (where 14 = E)

\section{Custom Circle Sizes}

\subsection{Smaller Circles (inner sep = 0.05)}

\[\sdiv[0.05,arrow]{97}{5}\]

\subsection{Default Circles (inner sep = 0.25)}

\[\sdiv[circle,arrow]{97}{5}\]

\subsection{Medium Circles (inner sep = 0.3)}

\[\sdiv[0.3,arrow]{97}{5}\]

\subsection{Larger Circles (inner sep = 0.5)}

\[\sdiv[0.5,arrow]{97}{5}\]

\section{Edge Cases}

\subsection{Dividend Less Than Divisor}

When the dividend is smaller than the divisor:
\[\sdiv[circle]{3}{10}\]

\subsection{Dividend Equal to Divisor}

\[\sdiv[circle]{7}{7}\]

\subsection{Dividend is Zero}

\[\sdiv[circle]{0}{5}\]

\subsection{Single Division Step}

\[\sdiv[circle,arrow]{10}{3}\]

\section{Large Numbers}

\subsection{Million}

\[\sdiv[circle,arrow]{1000000}{7}\]

\subsection{Large Dividend, Small Divisor}

\[\sdiv[circle]{9876543}{11}\]

\subsection{Ten Factorial}

$10! = 3628800$

\[\sdiv{3628800}{11}\]

\section{In-line Usage}

Division can be used in-line as well: $\sdiv{42}{5}$ produces a diagram within the text, though displayed equations ($\backslash[\ldots\backslash]$) are recommended for better readability.
